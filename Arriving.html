Getting Here

How do most visitors arrive on Taniti?

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes.

Did you know?

Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.


Taniti is the ideal tropical destination, offering the perfect backdrop for memorable family vacations and honeymoons, as well as unique venues for destination weddings and business trips. With its diverse terrain spanning sandy beaches to a volcanic interior, every visitor finds their perfect escape on our island.
