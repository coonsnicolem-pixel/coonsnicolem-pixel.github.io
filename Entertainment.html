Entertainment

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano. However, there are other things to do!

For the adventurer:

  • Ziplining in the rainforest
  • Going on chartered fishing tours
  • Snorkeling
  • Taking helicopter rides

For the family:

  • Visiting a local history museum
  • Seeing a movie
  • Playing at an arcade
  • Visiting a local art gallery
  • Bowling

    • Also, a nine-hole golf course should be operational by next year.

Perfect for the life of the party:

  • Visiting several pubs
  • Exploring a microbrewery
  • Dancing at a new dance club

Did you know?

Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Taniti is the ideal tropical destination, offering the perfect backdrop for memorable family vacations and honeymoons, as well as unique venues for destination weddings and business trips. With its diverse terrain spanning sandy beaches to a volcanic interior, every visitor finds their perfect escape on our island.
