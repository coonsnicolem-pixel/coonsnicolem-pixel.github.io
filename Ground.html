Local Transport

How do most visitors get around Taniti?

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses serve the rest of the island. Taxis are available in Taniti City, and rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport. Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors (helmets are required by law). Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable.

Did you know?

Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing: this area is easy to explore on foot.

Taniti is the ideal tropical destination, offering the perfect backdrop for memorable family vacations and honeymoons, as well as unique venues for destination weddings and business trips. With its diverse terrain spanning sandy beaches to a volcanic interior, every visitor finds their perfect escape on our island.
